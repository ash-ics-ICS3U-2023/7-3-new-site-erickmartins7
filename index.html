Our History

Monster Truck Mayhem is an annual event that has been running since 2008. This monster truck show brings people from around the country together to watch daring stunts and listen to the roaring engines of monster trucks. On average, Monster Truck Mayhem attracts 45, 050 visitors of all ages annually.
